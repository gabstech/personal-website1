What I do

Autonomous & Grid-Connected Photovoltaic Installation Design

This service involves the precise dimensioning of photovoltaic installations for both off-grid (autonomous) and grid-connected systems. My approach includes an in-depth assessment of your energy needs, local solar resources, and system requirements to create an optimal, efficient, and cost-effective solar energy solution.

Hybrid Energy System Integration

Specializing in the design and installation of hybrid energy systems, this service merges the best of renewable energy technologies. Depending on your energy needs and location specifics, I will combine different energy sources (such as solar, wind, and hydro) to deliver a continuous, reliable, and sustainable power solution.

Combustion to Electric Vehicle Conversion

I offer comprehensive assistance to convert combustion engine vehicles into electric-powered ones. The process includes a thorough evaluation of the vehicle's specifications, selection of suitable electric components, and professional installation. This eco-friendly upgrade significantly reduces your vehicle's carbon footprint while maintaining, if not enhancing, performance and efficiency.
